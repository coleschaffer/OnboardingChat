⏺ Based on my exploration, here's the situation:

  The Good News: Core Automations Still Work

  When someone purchases without Typeform, the OnboardingChat completion is what
   triggers all the main automations:

  - Circle sync (both CA and SPG communities) ✅
  - ActiveCampaign sync (tags + lists for team members/partners) ✅
  - Slack welcome thread ✅
  - Monday.com sync (10 min delayed) ✅

  These are all triggered by completing OnboardingChat, not by Typeform. So the
  flow still works.

  What's Different/Missing

  1. The Slack welcome thread will be less rich - It normally includes Typeform
  data like:
    - Annual revenue
    - Revenue trend
    - Main challenge / what's holding them back
    - Why they applied for CA Pro
    - Decision timeline
    - How they heard about you (referral source)

  Without Typeform, Stefan won't see this context in the Slack thread.
  2. The "delayed welcome" fallback breaks - There's a safety net for buyers who
   skip OnboardingChat: 1 hour after purchase, the system sends a Slack welcome
  using Typeform data. Without Typeform data, this fallback can't generate
  meaningful content and gets skipped. But if they complete OnboardingChat
  (which they should after checkout redirect), this doesn't matter.
